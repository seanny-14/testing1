Editorial

Investigate officials higher than Zaldy Co

GOTCHA - Jarius Bondoc




Infrastructure scams begin with Malacañang’s annual budgeting. Kickbacks are inserted in funding proposals of
various agencies.


Foremost are Departments of Public Works, Education, Health, Agriculture and the
Interior and Local Government.


Filipinos ask the question: “Are we cursed by God” or being punished?


More payoffs are added during separate Senate and House committee hearings. Still more at the closed-door
bicameral conference, which finalizes the General Appropriations Acts for presidential signing.


That’s the interim report of the Independent Commission for Infrastructure. Filipinos have long known it.


But there’s a hole in ICI’s findings. It blames only resigned congressman Zaldy Co of Ako Bicol party-list.


It must dig deeper. One man alone, even if chairman of the House committee on appropriations, can’t be
responsible for P1.7-trillion overpriced flood works.


To begin with, the scandal spans two administrations. It began under Rody Duterte, 2016-2022, followed by
Bongbong Marcos, 2022 to present.


Co was appropriations boss from July 2022 to January 2025. Before him there were Davao City Rep Isidro Ungab,
July 2016-February 2020, and ACT-CIS party’s Eric Yap, March 2020-July 2022.


No one becomes head of that almighty committee unless handpicked by the Speaker. There’s also the counterpart
committee head at the Senate.


Also, no allocation can be inserted in a department’s budget without the secretary’s nod. Two of them, in fact,
are now squealing – but only on Co.
