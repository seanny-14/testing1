Meet the Team: MCU Supremos
Taekwondo Team – Kicking with
Discipline and Pride

November 12, 2025




The MCU Supremos Taekwondo Team stands as a testament to discipline, precision, and the indomitable fighting
spirit of every Centralino. With every kick and stance, they embody the values of respect, perseverance, and
excellence — hallmarks of true Supremos.


Their recent participation in the 2025 PTA-CAMANAVA Interschool Taekwondo Championships showcased their dedication and
skill, bringing home impressive results that continue to make the MCU community proud.


Representing Manila Central University with honor, these athletes remind us that strength is not only measured in power,
but also in focus, respect, and heart.


The MCU Supremos Taekwondo Team continues to uphold the purple and gold banner with every match — showing that in every
arena, Centralinos fight with courage and integrity.
