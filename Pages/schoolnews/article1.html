Meet the Team: MCU Supremos
Men’s Volleyball Team – Power
in Every Play

November 3, 2025




The MCU Supremos Men’s Volleyball Team represents strength, resilience, and the fighting heart of the Centralino spirit.
Known for their explosive plays and unwavering focus, the team continues to make their mark in UCAL Season 8, opening
the season with an impressive victory over Diliman College.


Every spike, block, and serve tells the story of hard work and determination. Behind their success lies countless hours of
training and a shared commitment to excellence — both on and off the court.


As they carry the purple and gold, the MCU Supremos Men’s Volleyball Team stands united in one goal: to play with pride,
perform with passion, and inspire every Centralino to believe in the power of teamwork.
The fight continues — Sugod, Supremos!
